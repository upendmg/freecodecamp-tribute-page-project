Narendra Modi

from tea-seller to prime minister of world's largest democracy INDIA

Narendra Modi is the current prime minister of INDIA

Journey of his life:

  • He was born on 17 September 1950 to a family of grocers in Vadnagar, Mehsana district,Gujrat.He was the third of six children born to his parents.As a child, Modi helped his father sell tea at the Vadnagar railway station, and later ran a tea stall with his brother near a bus terminus.At age eight, Modi discovered the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), and began attending its training.In 1978 Modi became an RSS regional organiser.n 1988, Modi was elected organising secretary of the party's Gujarat unit, marking his entrance into electoral politics.On 3 October 2001 he became the cheif minister of Gujrat.Modi played a significant role in the BJP's 2009 general-election campaign.Modi was sworn in as Prime Minister of India on 26 May 2014 at the Rashtrapati Bhavan.
